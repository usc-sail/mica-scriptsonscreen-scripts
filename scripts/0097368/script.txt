Slowly, a sound fades in. Low and indistinct at first,
until it becomes the buzzing of an insect. As the
sound grows louder, a vague image takes shape in the
distance: a FLY appears weaving its way to the foreground.
As it gets closer, it moves out of frame, only to re-enter,
CLOSER and LOUDER each time. Other less distinct sounds
become audible as the fly approaches. These sounds become
recognizable as MUFFLED VOICES and BREATHING, still
unclear, due to the LOUD BUZZING of the fly, now quite
large in the frame. The buzzing is blaringly loud as
the FLY abruptly exits again, leaving the frame BLACK
and SILENT. A moment later, the huge, monstrous face of
the fly SMASHES into frame filling it entirely.
