Only Lovers Left Alive
Set against the romantic desolation of Detroit and Tangiers,
an underground musician, deeply depressed by the direction
of human activities, reunites with his resilient and enigmatic
lover.
Their love story has already endured several centuries at
least, but their debauched idyll is soon disrupted by her wild
and uncontrollable younger sister.
Can these wise but fragile outsiders continue to survive as
the modern world collapses around them?
