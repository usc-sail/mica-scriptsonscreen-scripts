This tortured village is now held by the U.S. Army: men,
vehicles, movement. The EVENING SKY is filled with planes,
all ours, soaring overhead with impunity.
We drift through, as G.I.'s and vehicles cross our frame.
Then we CRANE UP to take in all of Cielblanc: 20 houses, an
empty gallows, a church in ruins. And a very toppled tower.
One French village liberated, another few hundred to go. In
the distance, the moon rises over the Normandy Coast. We...
FADE OUT...
